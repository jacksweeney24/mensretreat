The 6 Pillars

1. PONDER

It is often blindspots and subconscious beliefs and stories from the past that are keeping us from living the lives we want.

Through journaling prompts, group discussions, and deep introspection, we will reflect on our past, connect with our inner child, forgive past actions of ourselves and of others, and let go of what is keeping us from living our dream lives as our highest selves.

2. PRESENCE

The present moment is the key to unlocking a beautiful relationship with life.

We will drop deeply into Presence through techniques like mindful awareness, meditation, silent walks, and unconditional love to let go of stuck emotions and stories from the past that keep us from being fully present in our daily lives.

You will carry these techniques with you for the rest of your life.

3. PLAY

Remember when you were a kid and had unlimited energy? You could play all day and never feel tired.

That energy is still within you.

Through sports, games, exercise, and exploring creative movement, you will unlock the playfulness within you that will fuel the creation of your desired business, relationships, and lifestyle.

4. POTENTIAL

From this elevated frequency and through letting go of the ceilings we've placed on ourselves via conditioning, programming, and stories, our potential shines forth.

Who could you be, and what would you create without fear? Without scarcity? Without seeking external validation?

You will uncover these answers during the retreat.

5. POWER

Every man has a Divine King within him. Many of us have just forgotten.

Through exercises and discussions around Divine Masculinity and the King archetype, our aim is to empower you to reclaim your intrinsic Power, step into your King energy, claim yourself as the Hero of your own journey, and write the story for your future.

6. PURPOSE

Once our internal state is elevated to a higher frequency than ever before (via pondering, playing, and becoming present), and we realize who we could be as our highest selves (our potential), our purpose becomes clear.

Everyone on this earth has a purpose. And as a man, your purpose is your #1 priority.

Our mission is to provide the container and connections for you to unlock your deepest purpose, your reason for being on this earth.
